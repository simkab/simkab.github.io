Quality

The top priority of SIMKAB's quality policy is the satisfaction of all customers.

The basic prerequisite for this is the unrestricted fulfillment of all agreed customer demands. These include delivery reliability, cost awareness and quality.

The focus of our quality management is on avoiding mistakes. Through the continuous flow of the products through our competent quality assurance, we can meet our own requirements and those of our customers. To do so, we plan, control and monitor all business processes that have an impact on quality with:

  1. Quantity contracts and delivery schedules
  2. %100 final inspection of all items that leave our facility
  3. Tracebility of all orders
  4. Multiple calibrated test equipment
  5. Certified quality management system according to ISO 9001:2005
