In 2001, SIEMENS Turkey outsurced the wire harness unit and SIMKAB's business activities had started by our general manager Ahmet Cakmak (who was the director of the production in SIEMENS at that time) only with the telecommunication unit of SIEMENS Turkey.

SIMKAB has been involved in business with more than 40 other OEMs (more than 25% are well-known multinational companies) for 20 years. SIMKAB's activity areas are design and manufacturing of wire harnesses, and the company has continously expanded the product portfolio on wire harnesses since it's existence..

SIMKAB has in-house production and engineering departments to provide high-quality production in 3000 square meters indoor area in Istanbul/Turkey.

SIMKAB has recently launched it's e-commerce platform to sell cables, connectors and electronic components on electrobasket.com

Basics of Reliable Wire Harness

Most of industries that require electronic equipment uses wire harness to keep different electrical wires safely on indoor and outdoor settings. Before the installation of wire harness within an electronic equipment, it has to satisfy use-case based requirements to avoid any damage either during the production line or at the field.
